The Gull group has openings for postdocs, PhD students, and master/bachelor students at the University of Warsaw as part of the ERC Advanced grant *Quantum Algorithms*. Contact [emanuel.gull@gmail.com](mailto:emanuel.gull@gmail.com) to express interest early — funding agency paperwork requires lead time.

---

## Postdoctoral Positions

Several postdoc positions are available. Candidates must hold a PhD at the time of employment. Relevant backgrounds include computational or analytical condensed matter theory, applied mathematics, quantum chemistry, and high-performance computing. Experience with modern numerical or analytic methods in quantum theory is desired.

Formal applications respond to the official Euraxess posting: [https://euraxess.ec.europa.eu/jobs/393041](https://euraxess.ec.europa.eu/jobs/393041). Positions are advertised on a rolling basis — if none is currently listed, please reach out directly.

---

## PhD Positions

PhD candidates join the [Graduate School at the University of Warsaw](https://szkolydoktorskie.uw.edu.pl/en/sdnsip/enrolment/). Further information is available at the [physics department doctoral studies page](https://www.fuw.edu.pl/doctoral-studies.html).

Typical application timeline:
1. Application to the Doctoral School: mid-April
2. Recommendation letters: early June
3. Interview and entrance exam: June–August
4. Admission decisions: early August
5. Start: late September
