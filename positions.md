The Gull group has openings for postdocs, PhD students, and master/bachelor students at the University of Warsaw as part of the ERC Advanced grant *Quantum Algorithms*. Contact [emanuel.gull@gmail.com](mailto:emanuel.gull@gmail.com) to express interest early — funding agency paperwork requires lead time.

---

## Postdoctoral Positions

Postdoctoral positions are available for candidates with a PhD in condensed matter theory, electronic structure and materials theory, applied mathematics, or a related field. We are looking for researchers with strong background in numerical or analytical methods for quantum many-body systems, and an interest in developing new algorithms as well as applying them to physical problems. Positions are typically funded for three years.

To apply or express interest, contact [emanuel.gull@gmail.com](mailto:emanuel.gull@gmail.com) with a CV and a brief description of your research interests.

---

## PhD Positions

PhD positions are fully funded through the ERC Advanced grant *Quantum Algorithms*. PhD candidates join the [Graduate School at the University of Warsaw](https://szkolydoktorskie.uw.edu.pl/en/sdnsip/enrolment/). Further information is available at the [physics department doctoral studies page](https://www.fuw.edu.pl/doctoral-studies.html).

Typical application timeline:
1. Application to the Doctoral School: mid-April
2. Recommendation letters: early June
3. Interview and entrance exam: June–August
4. Admission decisions: early August
5. Start: late September
