3. The instructions for travel are as follows:
   1. Download the permission form from https://www.fuw.edu.pl/dokumenty-i-formularze.html, fill it in and submit it
   2. Google the preferred flight
   3. Contact WhyNotTravel at [email](mailto:uw@whynottravel.pl) for a quote and ensure the prices are similar
   4. Fill in the ticket requisition form at https://www.fuw.edu.pl/dokumenty-i-formularze.html using the WhyNotTravel booking reference
   5. Get the ticket requisition form signed. Once signed, order the ticket.
